### What has happened so far:

This year has been really interesting. When this year started I had a goal in mind. Comes August or September I will have another job. The place that I was working at was good, comfortable and easy. I got along with my employers and a lot of the employees but I was ready for a change.

One day I had just recently got to work and I was busy setting up everything for the day. I got a message from a friend saying "My uncle has an IT company and I recommend you, would you be interested?". Without hesistation I immediatly asked what the next steps were. Now here I am. 

I started working as an IT Software Engineer on the 2nd of February. I had already achieved a goal I had set out in the beginning of the year, so now what?

That is what this blog is for. This year I want change and so far its looking quite good. Throughout the coming weeks I will be posting weekly logs, notes and progress updates. I have a few new goals in mind but that will all be laid out in the coming weeks.
